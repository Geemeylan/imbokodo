Frequently asked

How long does delivery take?

Orders are dispatched within 3–5 business days. Delivery within South Africa typically takes 5–7 working days via courier. Each item is handmade, so please allow for slight variations in turnaround.

Do you ship internationally?

Not yet, but we're working on it. Currently we deliver within South Africa only. Sign up for our newsletter to be notified when international shipping launches.

How does the artisan receive payment?

100% of the product price goes directly to the artisan who made your item. Payments are processed within 48 hours of order confirmation and sent via mobile money or bank transfer.

Can I return or exchange an item?

Because each piece is handmade and unique, we do not accept returns for change of mind. If an item arrives damaged, please contact us within 7 days with photos and we will arrange a replacement.

What is B-BBEE procurement and how does it apply?

Bulk purchases from Imbokodo may qualify for B-BBEE Enterprise and Supplier Development credits under South Africa's Broad-Based Black Economic Empowerment framework. Contact us for documentation and compliance support.
